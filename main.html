About Us

Elite Energy Solutions is a trusted name in Electrical Contracting and Renewable Energy with a legacy that spans more than six decades. Established in the 1960s as a family-run electrical contracting business, the company has proudly carried forward four generations of expertise, earning a reputation for technical excellence, reliability, and integrity.

While our roots remain firmly in electrical contracting, our vision has evolved to meet the changing energy needs of South Africa. Today, we deliver integrated solutions across Commercial & Industrial PV Solar, Electrical Contracting, Automation, and Energy Efficiency, making us a comprehensive partner for businesses and industries seeking smarter, more sustainable power.

Electrical Contracting

Our electrical division remains the backbone of the company, delivering projects ranging from new installations and upgrades to specialized industrial systems and ongoing maintenance. Decades of contracting experience ensure every project is completed to the highest standards of safety, compliance, and durability.

Commercial & Industrial PV Solar

We design, install, and maintain large-scale grid-tied, hybrid, and off-grid solar power systems tailored to the unique needs of commercial and industrial clients. With proven expertise in Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS) and advanced inverter technologies, we help businesses reduce energy costs, stabilize operations, and achieve long-term sustainability goals.

Automation

From intelligent building controls to advanced energy management systems, our automation solutions allow businesses to optimize performance, reduce waste, and improve efficiency. By integrating automation with electrical and solar systems, we enable smarter operations that adapt to the demands of modern industry.

Energy Efficiency

We help our clients identify and implement strategies that lower consumption, improve power quality, and maximize ROI. Through energy audits, efficient system design, and tailored solutions, we enable businesses to cut operating costs while contributing to a cleaner energy future.

At Elite Energy Solutions, we pride ourselves on delivering honest advice, technically sound solutions, and measurable value. Our strength lies in blending a rich electrical heritage with forward-thinking innovation—ensuring that every project is not only fit for today but also future-ready.

For us, it's not just about power—it's about building resilient energy systems that empower businesses, strengthen industries, and support South Africa's energy transition.
